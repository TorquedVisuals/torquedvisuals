About the Author's Work

Lauren Glor’s work goes beyond mere documentation of the physical world; it captures the soul of her subjects. Her keen eye for subtle details allows her to uncover beauty in unexpected places, transforming everyday moments into powerful visual experiences. Whether she’s photographing nature, architecture, or human interactions, Lauren approaches each scene with a sense of discovery, constantly seeking out new perspectives to present familiar subjects in fresh and intriguing ways. Her ability to find beauty in the smallest elements—like the reflection of light on a surface or the way a shadow falls—sets her apart as a true visual storyteller. Her portfolio reflects a diverse range of styles, from meticulously composed still life images to candid street photography that invites viewers to feel as though they’ve been transported into the moment. Through her work, Lauren aims to create an emotional connection, allowing her audience to not only admire the technical precision of her photographs but also to feel the atmosphere and energy she captures. Each image serves as a testament to her dedication to both the art of photography and the stories she strives to tell. By always pushing her creative boundaries, Lauren ensures her work remains fresh, exciting, and thought-provoking.

About the Author's Equipment

Lauren uses primarily Canon products, including her Canon T5, T8i, and Canon Powershot, relying on their high-quality performance for both photography and videography. She appreciates the clarity and detail Canon cameras offer, allowing her to capture the finest nuances in every shot, from the sharpness of a subject's features to the subtle gradation of light in a scene. For videography, she uses a DJI Osmo 3, a versatile tool that ensures smooth, cinematic footage even in motion. This stabilizer has become an essential part of her kit, enabling her to produce professional-level videos with a fluid, polished look, regardless of the environment. The portability and ease of use of the Osmo 3 make it perfect for capturing spontaneous moments while maintaining the high visual standards that Lauren strives for in her work. The combination of these trusted tools empowers Lauren to consistently deliver dynamic, high-quality content, whether shooting stills or videos.
