About the Author

My name is Lauren Glor, and I am 20 years old. I grew up in Miami, Florida, where I spent most of my childhood surrounded by the vibrant energy of the city before moving to Georgia. Currently, I am pursuing my passion for design as an Interaction Design major with a minor in Marketing at Kennesaw State University.My diverse interests and talents come together in many aspects of my life, with my entrepreneurial spirit leading me to launch my own photography business, Torqued Visuals. Starting Torqued Visuals was a natural progression for me, as it blends my love for colors, which stemmed from my deep appreciation for art, with my passion for cars. Photography has always been a way for me to express myself creatively, and it’s amazing how I can capture the world through my lens, combining these influences into something unique. I find joy in balancing my artistic side with my academic and professional pursuits, constantly striving to bring a sense of purpose and creativity to everything I do.
